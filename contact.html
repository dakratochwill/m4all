Location and Directions

Medicines for All is located in the Biotech 8 Research Facility within the Virginia BIotechnology Research Park.

737 North 5th Street
Richmond, Virginia 23219

Please note that the entrance to the building is on Jackson Street between 5th Street and Navy Hill Drive. Parking is available in the deck directly behind the building. If you are a VCU parking permit holder, you can feel free to park in any of the spots on the right-hand side of the second floor of the deck marked “VCU College of Engineering.” If you are not a VCU parking permit holder, please contact us (m4all@vcu.edu) to organize a guest parking pass.

Take Exit #190 for Fifth Street and Downtown/Coliseum. Merge onto 5th Street. Take first left onto E. Jackson Street. Turn left onto N. 8th Street. The 8th Street Parking Deck is on the right. To walk to the IEM, exit parking deck and turn right on Jackson Street. Walk three blocks and turn right on 5th Street. The Biotech Eight Facility is at N. 5th Street and Jackson Street.

Take the 3rd Street exit toward Coliseum/Downtown.Turn left on E. Jackson Street. Turn left on N. 8th Street. The 8th Street Parking Deck is on the right. To walk to the IEM, exit parking deck and turn right on Jackson Street. Walk three blocks and turn right on 5th Street. The Biotech Eight Facility is at N. 5th Street and Jackson Street.

From I-95 South, use the right lane to keep right at the fork, continue on I-64 and follow signs for Williamsburg/Norfolk. From I-64, take the 3rd Street exit toward Coliseum/Downtown. Turn left on E. Jackson Street. Turn left on N. 8th Street. The 8th Street Parking Deck is on the right. To walk to the IEM, exit parking deck and turn right on Jackson Street. Walk three blocks and turn right on 5th Street. The Biotech Eight Facility is at N. 5th Street and Jackson Street.

From I-95 South, take exit 74C and merge onto E. Broad Street. Take first right onto College Street. Take first left onto E. Marshall Street. Take first right onto N. 13th Street. N. 13th Street becomes E. Duval Street. Continue on Duval Street to roundabout. Exit roundabout on N. 8th Street. The 8th Street Parking Deck is on the left. To walk to the IEM, exit parking deck and turn right on Jackson Street. Walk three blocks and turn right on 5th Street. The Biotech Eight Facility is at N. 5th Street and Jackson Street.

VCU students, faculty and staff members can use the VCU Campus Connector busses to access the Institute for Engineering and Medicine’s Biotech Eight facility. Take the Connector to 3rd Street and E. Marshall Street. Walk 2 blocks north on 3rd Street. Turn right on Jackson Street and walk a block and a half. The Biotech Eight facility is at the intersection of Jackson Street and 5th Street.
